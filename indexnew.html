What is PeakCY?

PeakCY is a community for people in Protaras who want more than small talk. We bring together ambitious, open-minded, and team-oriented individuals who enjoy sharing ideas, staying active, and exploring the island together.

We’re filling the gap with real meetups, real friendships, and a culture of “sharing is caring.”

The Vibe & Values

Relaxed, adventurous, supportive. We give and we grow together.

  • Positive energy only: we’re relaxed, adventurous, and team-spirited.
  • Life-wide connections: meet people who share your lifestyle, fitness, and passions.
  • Share to grow: exchange knowledge (fitness, health, business) and lift each other up.
  • Invite-only: new members are vouched in by existing ones. Quality people, zero spam.
  • Cyprus love: rooted in the island’s beaches, mountains, and Mediterranean life.

This Isn’t For

  • Blamers: if you don’t take responsibility, this isn’t for you.
  • Energy drainers: chronic complainers or takers who don’t give back.
  • Sales pitches: no course funnels or pushy promotions. Community first.
  • Talkers: people who talk the talk but never walk the walk.
  • Excuse-makers: those who constantly find reasons why not.

What We Actually Do

  • Casual hangouts: cafés, dinners, and family-friendly gatherings.
  • Adventures across Cyprus: guided hikes and island explorations (led by our certified guide).
  • Fitness that connects: member-led workouts — beach bootcamps, gym sessions, yoga.
  • Mastermind dinners (coming soon): themed nights and guest talks on growth and life.

We meet in person every month — building trust, connection, and something real, together.

Who Fits Here

You’ll fit in if you’re growth-focused and social — whether you run a business, coach, or work in tech, fitness, or wellness.

You’re team-spirited, enjoy giving as much as you get, and want to grow with others.

What Makes PeakCY Different

PeakCY isn’t about sales. It’s real people helping each other rise — socially, personally, and professionally. No fluff. No pressure. Just community.

FAQ

Is PeakCY just for business networking?

No. It’s about finding your people — mixing social life, fitness, and growth through real relationships.

How often are meetups?

One main meetup per month, plus optional hangouts like hikes, gym sessions, and dinners.

Is it invite-only?

Yes. All members are approved or vouched for — to keep the vibe high and authentic.

Why focus on Cyprus?

We’re based in Protaras and proud of it — beaches, mountains, Mediterranean life. We’re building a strong local community first.
