What is PeakCY?

PeakCY is a social club for people in the Famagusta / Protaras area who want more than small talk. We connect ambitious, open, team-oriented people who like to socialize, learn from each other, and explore Cyprus together.

United Community

Meet people who actually vibe with your lifestyle — gym, outdoors, family-friendly hangs, and good energy.

Curated, Not Random

Invite-only with member vouching. Quality people, zero spam. Community first — not a course funnel.

Cyprus Pride

Rooted in Cyprus — beaches, mountains, Mediterranean life. We’re building something real here in Famagusta / Protaras.

There wasn’t a real community hub here — just lots of individuals looking for their crowd. We’re bridging that gap with real meetups, real friendships, and a culture of “sharing is caring.”
Networking beyond business: find your people, then grow together — socially, personally, and professionally.
We start local and keep it high-quality. Collaborations and masterclasses will come once the core is strong.

Welcome to PeakCY.

The Vibe & Values

Relaxed, adventurous, supportive — we give and we grow together.

✓ You Belong If You Are
  • Team-spirited — sharing is caring; you give as much as you get.
  • Open & curious — eager to learn (fitness, health, lifestyle, business).
  • Social & active — you like hikes, gym, dinners, and good company.
  • Goal-oriented — you want to grow with like-minded people.
  • Positive — good vibes only; we keep the culture strong.
× This Isn't For
  • Upsell hunters — this is not a course or sales funnel.
  • Energy drainers — we keep it supportive and constructive.
  • Gatekeepers — knowledge hoarders instead of contributors.
  • Serial no-shows — reliability matters in a small community.
  • Bad-vibe behavior — disrespect kills the room; not welcome.
