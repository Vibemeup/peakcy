What is PeakCY?

PeakCY is a social club for people in the Protaras / Famagusta area who want more than small talk. We connect ambitious, open, team-oriented people who like to socialize, learn from each other, and explore Cyprus together.

There wasn’t a real community hub here — just lots of individuals looking for their crowd. We’re bridging that gap with real meetups, real friendships, and a culture of “sharing is caring.”

The Vibe & Values

Relaxed, adventurous, supportive. We give and we grow together.

  • Good vibes first: relaxed, supportive, adventurous, easy-going.
  • Networking beyond business: meet people who share your lifestyle, gym habits, and interests.
  • Give & receive: share what you know (fitness, health, lifestyle, business) — we all rise together.
  • Curated membership: Carefully Curated. Invite-only by other member vouching. Quality people, zero spam. Only the real deal!
  • Local pride: rooted in Cyprus — beaches, mountains, Mediterranean life. We rep our island.

This Isn’t For

  • Blamers: those who blame others instead of taking responsibility.
  • Drainers: people who drain energy instead of contributing to it.
  • Spam & funnels: Community first. Not a course funnel.

What We Actually Do

  • Casual meetups: bars, cafés, family/kid-friendly hangs, and dinners.
  • Outdoors: hikes, mini-trips, and guided days out across Cyprus (we have a pro guide in the group).
  • Fitness together: member-led sessions for bonding and sweat (many of us train hard and care about health).
  • Mastermind dinners (soon): once the core is solid, we’ll run themed nights and guest masterclasses.

We meet monthly, in person, to grow closer, climb higher, and build something real. This is how the PeakCY tribe begins — together, on the ground, lifting each other up.

Who Fits Here

Typically 30–50, entrepreneurial or entrepreneurial-minded (from Airbnb rentals and coaching to supplements, fitness, or AI), goal-oriented, social, and Team-spirited. If you like to give as much as you get — you’ll feel at home.

FAQ

Is PeakCY only for business networking?

No. It’s networking in the wider sense — finding your people in Cyprus. We mix social life, fitness, and growth with real relationships.

How often are meetups?

One anchor meetup per month, plus optional spontaneous hangs (hikes, gym sessions, dinners).

Is it invite-only?

Yes — applications are reviewed, and existing members can vouch friends. This keeps it curated and high-vibe.

Why Cyprus-specific?

We’re based in Protaras and proud of it — beaches, mountains, Mediterranean lifestyle. We want to grow an awesome community here and showcase Cyprus to the world.

Do you do collabs with local places?

We’re building the core first. Collabs with gyms, restaurants, and hotels will roll in as we grow.
